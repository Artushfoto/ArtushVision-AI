# 🔑 OpenRouter API Key Setup Guide

Follow this step-by-step guide to register, generate your secure API key, and configure your AI engine for ArtushVision AI.

---

## 01. How to Get Your OpenRouter API Key
Create your account and generate a secure access token in just a few minutes.

1. **Visit the Website:** Navigate to the [OpenRouter.ai](https://openrouter.ai/) platform.
2. **Registration:** Click on **Sign Up** and register using your email address and a secure password.
   * ![Registration Step](https://via.placeholder.com/800x100?text=Click+to+Sign+Up+Placeholder)
3. **Confirm Your Email:** Check your inbox and click the verification link in the confirmation email from OpenRouter.
4. **Create Your API Key:** * Navigate to the **Keys** section in your dashboard.
   * Click the **"Create Key"** button.
   * Give your key a recognizable name (e.g., `ArtushVision AI`).
   * ![Create Key](https://via.placeholder.com/800x100?text=Create+Your+Free+API+Key+Placeholder)

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Save Your Key Immediately!**
> Copy the generated key and store it in a password manager or a secure document. For security reasons, **you will not be able to see this key again** once you close the window.

---

## 02. Understanding Usage (Free vs. Paid)

OpenRouter offers flexibility depending on your volume and required precision.

### 🆓 Free Models
* **Accessibility:** Explore 25+ free models.
* **Limits:** Typically limited to 50 requests per day.
* **Performance:** *Please note that free models may work intermittently or experience higher latency during peak hours.*

### 💰 Paid Usage (Highly Recommended for Professionals)
For high-volume stock photography or large archives, purchasing credits is the most stable and cost-effective solution.

* **Minimum Deposit:** 5 USD (+ local VAT).
* **Extreme Efficiency:** With a $5 credit, you can describe approximately **100,000 – 150,000 photos** using optimized models.
* **Recommended Model:** `google/gemini-2.0-flash-001`
* ![Buy Credits](https://via.placeholder.com/800x100?text=Buy+Credits+on+OpenRouter+Placeholder)

> [!TIP]
> **Why Gemini 2.0 Flash?**
> This model offers the best "intelligence-to-price" ratio. It is exceptionally fast and capable of generating detailed, SEO-friendly descriptions even for complex subjects while keeping your per-image cost near zero.

## ⚙️ Next Steps
Once you have copied your key:
1. Open **ArtushVision AI**.
2. Go to **File > API Key**.
3. Paste your key and click **Save**.
4. You are now ready to run your first AI analysis!
